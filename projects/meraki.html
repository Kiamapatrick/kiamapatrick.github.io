Meraki

Description

Meraki is a professional-looking site for car sales and servicing businesses. It includes a homepage featuring services, featured cars, and contact information. The website was designed to be simple, responsive, and user-friendly—perfect for auto shops or dealerships looking to expand their digital footprint.

A passionate site for car sales and servicing.

Tech Used

  • 💻 HTML5 & CSS3
  • ⚙️ Bootstrap 5 (for layout and cards)
  • 🖱️ JavaScript form logic or validation
  • 📱 Responsive-first layout design
