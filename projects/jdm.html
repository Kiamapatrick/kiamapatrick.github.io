JDM Culture

Description

A tribute to Japanese Domestic Market (JDM) car culture. This site highlights legendary tuners like Top Secret, showcases iconic builds, and celebrates performance-driven design and visuals. Tailored for car enthusiasts.

Tech Used

  • 💻 HTML, CSS (Flexbox & Grid)
  • 🎨 Google Fonts & FontAwesome
  • 🖼️ Lightbox JS (or custom JS for image viewing)
  • 🛠️ SimpleJS animations
